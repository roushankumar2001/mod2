OUR MENU

Pasta

Pasta is the Italian designation . It is a dish typically made from grain flour, commonly wheat, mixed into a paste or dough, usually with water or eggs, and formed or cut into sheets or other shapes. It is usually cooked by boiling, baking or frying.

Samosa

A samosa is a fried or baked pastry with a savoury filling, such as spiced potatoes, onions, peas, cheese, beef and other meats, or lentils. It may take different forms, including triangular, cone, or half-moon shapes, depending on the region.

Omlet

Omlet is a dish made from beaten eggs , fried with butter or oil in a frying pan.It is quite common for the omelette to be folded around fillings such as cheese, chives, vegetables, mushrooms, or some combination of the above.
